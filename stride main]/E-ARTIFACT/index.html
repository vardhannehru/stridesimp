E-ARTIFACT

  • 2 MARCH, 2024
  • Online Event

E ARTIFACT, an online technical event hosted by the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) branch specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI&ML) at Pragati Engineering College, offers an exciting platform for participants. With various technical competitions, students from diverse backgrounds can engage in challenging tasks, showcasing their skills. The event provides an opportunity for all to earn an e-certificate and attractive rewards. As a significant technical gathering, E ARTIFACT reflects the commitment of Pragati Engineering College to nurturing innovation and excellence within the AI&ML community. It serves as a valuable experience for participants to enhance their technical prowess and collaborate with like-minded individuals. Joining E ARTIFACT not only broadens knowledge but also opens doors to recognition and achievement in the dynamic field of AI&ML..
